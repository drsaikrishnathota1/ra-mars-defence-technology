# Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editor-in-Chief,

I am pleased to submit the manuscript entitled “RA-MARS: A Resilient AI-Driven Mission Assurance Framework for Secure Multi-UAV Defence Surveillance Under Jamming, GPS/GNSS Spoofing, and Data-Tampering Attacks” for consideration in Defence Technology.

This manuscript proposes RA-MARS, a defence-oriented mission assurance framework for secure multi-UAV surveillance in contested environments. The framework integrates AI-based attack detection, mission-risk scoring, adaptive mission-continuation logic, and blockchain-inspired tamper-resistant mission logging to address radio-frequency jamming, GPS/GNSS spoofing, and mission-data tampering.

A simulation-based evaluation was conducted using synthetic multi-UAV telemetry data under normal, jamming, GPS/GNSS spoofing, data-tampering, and combined attack scenarios. Using row-level attack labels and non-leakage telemetry features, the best-performing model achieved 89.87% accuracy, 90.50% macro precision, 89.86% macro recall, and 89.71% macro F1-score. Under the combined attack scenario, RA-MARS improved mission success rate from 63.21% to 78.60%, increased packet delivery ratio from 0.761 to 0.885, reduced average route deviation from 67.32 m to 48.00 m, and reduced mission recovery time from 117.64 s to 67.97 s compared with the conventional UAV baseline.

The manuscript aligns with the scope of Defence Technology because it addresses secure and resilient autonomous surveillance systems for defence-relevant contested environments. The work contributes to defence science and engineering by connecting cyber-electromagnetic attack detection, mission-risk assessment, adaptive mission continuity, and trustworthy mission logging in a unified multi-UAV mission assurance framework.

This manuscript has not been published previously and is not under consideration for publication elsewhere. The author declares no competing financial interests or personal relationships that could have appeared to influence the work reported in this paper.

Thank you for considering this manuscript for publication in Defence Technology.

Kind regards,

Dr. Sai Krishna Thota
