# Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editor-in-Chief,

I am pleased to submit the manuscript entitled “RA-MARS: A Resilient AI-Driven Mission Assurance Framework for Secure Multi-UAV Defence Surveillance Under Jamming, GPS Spoofing, and Data-Tampering Attacks” for consideration in Defence Technology.

This manuscript proposes RA-MARS, a defence-oriented mission assurance framework for secure multi-UAV surveillance in contested environments. The framework integrates AI-based attack detection, mission-risk scoring, adaptive mission-continuation logic, and blockchain-inspired tamper-resistant mission logging to address radio-frequency jamming, GPS spoofing, and mission-data tampering.

The study is designed to evaluate UAV resilience not only through attack detection accuracy but also through mission success rate, packet delivery ratio, latency, energy consumption, tamper-detection rate, and mission recovery time. The proposed framework is compared with conventional UAV surveillance, AI-only detection, logging-only, and non-adaptive secure baselines.

The manuscript aligns with the scope of Defence Technology because it addresses secure and resilient autonomous surveillance systems for defence-relevant contested environments. The work contributes to defence science and engineering by connecting cyber-electromagnetic attack detection, mission-risk assessment, adaptive mission continuity, and trustworthy mission logging in a unified multi-UAV mission assurance framework.

This manuscript has not been published previously and is not under consideration for publication elsewhere. The author declares no competing financial interests or personal relationships that could have appeared to influence the work reported in this paper.

Thank you for considering this manuscript for publication in Defence Technology.

Kind regards,

Dr. Sai Krishna Thota
