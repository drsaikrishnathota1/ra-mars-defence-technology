## Claims That Require Evidence

Do not make these claims unless the simulation supports them:

- RA-MARS significantly improves mission success
- RA-MARS reduces mission recovery time
- RA-MARS outperforms all baselines
- RA-MARS maintains low latency
- RA-MARS is suitable for real-world deployment
- RA-MARS is military-grade

## Safe Claim Style

Use careful wording such as:

- "Simulation results indicate..."
- "The proposed framework demonstrates improved resilience under the tested scenarios..."
- "Compared with the selected baselines, RA-MARS shows better mission continuity..."
- "The findings suggest that integrated mission assurance can improve multi-UAV resilience..."
- "Further real-world validation is required before operational deployment."
