# UAV Defence Surveillance and Contested Environments Notes

## Purpose
This file collects references and notes related to UAV defence surveillance, contested environments, GPS spoofing, jamming, and mission assurance.

## Why This Category Matters
RA-MARS is positioned as a defence-oriented mission assurance framework. Therefore, the manuscript must show that multi-UAV surveillance systems are relevant to defence operations and vulnerable in contested electromagnetic and cyber-physical environments.

## Current Context
Recent public reports show that GPS spoofing and jamming are not only theoretical threats. For example, Lithuania reported in May 2026 that GPS spoofing capabilities from Kaliningrad had expanded significantly and could affect signals deep into Europe. This supports the real-world relevance of studying GPS spoofing and jamming resilience.
